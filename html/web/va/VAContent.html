All MITx tools were developed as prototypes, and were not meant to be final products. Many of the MITx prototypes later had their development continued and finished by other developers, after handing off the project to them.

This app was made during my time with MITx. The purpose was to give students a more interactive and engaging experience when watching lecture vidoes online.

  • Languages/Technologies used:
    • Front End:
      • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery
      • Bootstrap
      • YouTube API
    • Back End:
      • Parse.com
  • Features:
    • Video Annotations
      • Users can make comments in videos as you can with YouTube videos
      • Users can associate their comment with an area on a specific frame/frames in the video, by drawing out an area in the frame
      • Comments are also time stamped either at a specific time, or at a time range
    • Comment Heat Map
      • Below the player scrubbing controls, a heat map is visible
      • Each comment made is shown in the heat map as a tick
      • Hovering over the tick shows a preview of the comment
      • Clicking on the tick shows the comment in the comment side bar, and automatically takes the video to the time associated with the comment
      • The heat map is also zoomable, by dragging an area to zoom into
      • The zoomed area is draggable to zoom a different part of the heat map
